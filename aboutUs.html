ABOUT US

The First and #1 E-Commerce Platform for Farmers and Fishers
SARISARI PH is an e-commerce platform provider for farmers, fishers, and producers. Local farmers and fishers can sell their produce thus promoting local farming and fishery while providing healthier options to consumers and aiming to bridge the gap between agri-fisheries and direct market.
MISSION

To bridge the gap between agri-fisheries and direct market, while promoting healthier options to consumers.

VISION

The first and no. 1 e-commerce platform for farmers and fishers to the market.
